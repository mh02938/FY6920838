Introduction 

        The Lockheed Martin F35 Lightning II is the most advanced multi role fighter aircraft to ever exist, it combines unrivalled weapon systems with unparalleled stealth technology in order to achieve dominate the skies and remain almost undetectable to enemy forces. This paper will be focusing on the stealth technology used on the F35 Lightning II, specifically the radar countermeasures, examining these technologies independently and then how they work together to achieve such a high level of undetectability.
